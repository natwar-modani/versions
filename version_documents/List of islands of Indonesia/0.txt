indonesia includes more than 17,000 islands, 6,000 are inhabited.

major islands of indonesia:

 sumatra
 java
 borneo (kalimantan)
 sulawesi (formerly known as celebes)
 new guinea (eastern half is the independent country of papua new guinea)

other indonesian islands:

 madura
 halmahera
 amboinia

 nusa tenggara islands or lesser sunda islands
 bali
 sumbawa
 sumba
 flores
 lombok
 timor (eastern half is the independent country of east timor)

 maluku islands (or moluccas)
 banda islands
