harwich is a town in essex, england, located on the coast
with the north sea to the east.

nearby places include felixstowe to the northeast, ipswich to the
northwest and colchester to the southwest.

there is a regular ferry service between harwich and hoek van holland
in the netherlands.

the electric palace cinema, harwich, is one of the oldest purpose-built cinemas to survive complete with its silent screen, original projection room and ornamental frontage still intact.
